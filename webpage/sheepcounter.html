Affordable, low-hassle sleeptracking.

Sheepcounter is a wristmounted device for monitoring the duration and details of the user's sleep cycle. Using the principle of actigraphy implemented on affordable hardware, Sheepcounter will interface with a combination of social networking websites and logging software. In short, Sheepcounter is affordable, low-hassle sleeptracking.


Since we've ideated Sheepcounter, a lot of folks have had the same general idea: Lark, Fibit. More importantly, they're going for a more reasonable price point than what the market had before. We're not actively developing this product now: we're not sure there's room in the market for our potential device. As such, our original preliminary research can be found here.

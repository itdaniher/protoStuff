Analog circuit analysis done right.

Given the amount of processing power available in modern computers, it's puzzling as to why it's so difficult to use the ever-available computer to analyze circuits. Combining a power supply and multimeter with the resources of the personal computer, OpenBench provides an open-source and inexpensive substitute to a benchtop multimeter, oscilloscope and power supply. It is a tool that helps you analyze circuits quickly and efficiently - it is analog circuit analysis done right.
